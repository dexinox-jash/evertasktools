# Data Deletion Policy for EverTask

**Last Updated:** April 14, 2026

## Our Approach to Data

EverTask is an offline-first application. All data you create remains on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal information.

## How to Delete Your Data

### Option 1: Delete Individual Tasks
1. Open the EverTask App
2. Navigate to the task you wish to delete
3. Tap the menu icon and select **Archive** or **Delete**
4. For archived tasks, go to **History** and tap the delete icon to permanently remove them

### Option 2: Clear All App Data
1. Open your device's **Settings**
2. Go to **Apps** → **EverTask**
3. Tap **Storage**
4. Tap **Clear Data** or **Clear Storage**

This will immediately delete all tasks, subtasks, templates, and settings stored by the App.

### Option 3: Uninstall the App
Uninstalling EverTask from your device permanently deletes all local data associated with the App, including the SQLite database and any backup files in the App's private storage.

## Backup Files

If you have used the backup feature, a JSON backup file may exist in:
```
/Android/data/com.evertask.app/files/EverTask/backup.json
```

You can delete this file manually using your device's file manager. Uninstalling the App does not always delete files in external storage, so manual deletion may be necessary.

## Data We Retain

Because EverTask does not use cloud servers, we do not retain any of your data after you delete it from your device.
