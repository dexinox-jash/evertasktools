## 🛡️ Document-by-Document Protection Summary

### Privacy Policy
**Purpose:** Comply with PIPEDA + protect against privacy claims

**Your Risk Without It:**
- Privacy violation fines (up to $100,000 under PIPEDA)
- Class action lawsuits
- Regulatory investigations
- Reputational damage

**Protection Provided:**
- ✅ Documents zero data collection (your actual practice)
- ✅ PIPEDA compliance framework
- ✅ Provincial law compliance (QC, AB, BC)
- ✅ Data breach procedures (even though impossible)
- ✅ User rights disclosure
- ✅ Contact information for complaints

**Key Strength:** Since you collect NO data, privacy liability is essentially ZERO.

---

### Terms and Conditions
**Purpose:** Master agreement + maximum legal protection

**Your Risk Without It:**
- Unlimited liability for app failures
- Users claiming warranties
- Court lawsuits in unfavorable jurisdictions
- Class action exposure
- IP theft/copying

**Protection Provided:**
- ✅ License restrictions (no copying, reverse engineering)
- ✅ IP ownership protection
- ✅ User content responsibility (they own it, not you)
- ✅ Prohibited conduct definitions
- ✅ Termination rights
- ✅ AS-IS warranty disclaimer
- ✅ Liability capped at $4.99
- ✅ Indemnification by users
- ✅ Arbitration (avoids court)
- ✅ Class action waiver
- ✅ Your province's law governs

**Key Strength:** This is your primary legal shield.

---

### EULA
**Purpose:** Software license (App Store/Google Play requirement)

**Your Risk Without It:**
- Apple/Google may reject app
- Users think they "own" the software
- No protection for updates
- No third-party enforcement

**Protection Provided:**
- ✅ "Licensed, not sold" clarity
- ✅ Source code protection
- ✅ Updates covered
- ✅ Apple/Google can enforce (third-party beneficiaries)
- ✅ Export control compliance
- ✅ AS-IS disclaimer
- ✅ Liability limitation

**Key Strength:** Required by app stores; protects your IP.

---

### Sales Terms
**Purpose:** Purchase terms + strict refund policy

**Your Risk Without It:**
- Refund demands
- Payment disputes
- Chargebacks
- Consumer protection claims

**Protection Provided:**
- ✅ Clear one-time purchase (no subscriptions)
- ✅ CDN$4.99 pricing locked
- ✅ Apple/Google handle payments (not you)
- ✅ **NO REFUNDS policy**
- ✅ Only 2 narrow exceptions
- ✅ Technical requirements disclosed
- ✅ Updates included
- ✅ Consumer rights preserved (mandatory laws)

**Key Strength:** Transfers all payment/refund issues to Apple/Google.

## 🚨 What These Documents CAN'T Protect Against

### 1. **Willful Misconduct**
If you intentionally harm users, no contract protects you.

### 2. **Fraud**
If you misrepresent the app, documents are void.

### 3. **Gross Negligence**
Extreme carelessness may override liability caps.

### 4. **Statutory Violations**
If you violate consumer protection laws, documents may not help.

### 5. **Criminal Acts**
Documents don't protect against illegal activity.

### 6. **Unenforceable Clauses**
Courts may strike down certain provisions (varies by province).

## 🎓 Key Legal Concepts Explained

### **PIPEDA**
Canada's federal privacy law. Requires consent, purpose limitation, and breach notification.

### **Arbitration**
Private dispute resolution (cheaper/faster than court). You pick the arbitrator.

### **Indemnification**
User promises to defend you if they cause problems.

### **AS-IS Disclaimer**
You make no promises about quality or performance.

### **Liability Cap**
Maximum amount you can be forced to pay (here: CDN$4.99).

### **Class Action Waiver**
Users can't band together in large lawsuits.

### **Governing Law**
Which province's laws apply to disputes.
