## What is EverTask?

**EverTask** is a free, privacy-first productivity app for Android that helps you break down any task into simple, actionable steps using your voice or text. It features smart templates, visual progress tracking, exact-alarm reminders, and beautiful home-screen widgets — all without collecting a single byte of your personal data.

- **Platform:** Android (Google Play)
- **Price:** Free
- **Data Policy:** 100% offline — all data stays on your device
- **Accounts:** None required

## 🔒 Our Privacy Promise

- **No data collection.** We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal information.
- **No cloud required.** All tasks, templates, and backups are stored locally on your device.
- **No tracking.** We do not use analytics, advertising identifiers, or third-party trackers.
- **Your control.** You can delete all data at any time within the app, via Android settings, or by uninstalling.
