## 🔒 Key Protections Built Into These Documents

### 1. **Zero Data Collection = Zero Privacy Liability**
Since your app collects NO data:
- No data breach risk
- No privacy violation exposure
- Minimal PIPEDA obligations
- Users can't claim data misuse

### 2. **Liability Cap at $4.99**
All documents limit your liability to the purchase price:
- User can't sue for millions
- Even if app causes data loss
- Even if app malfunctions
- Maximum exposure: $4.99 per user

### 3. **No Refund Policy**
Sales Terms explicitly state:
- ALL SALES ARE FINAL
- No refunds (go to Apple/Google)
- Only 2 narrow exceptions
- Protects your revenue

### 4. **Arbitration Clause**
Disputes must go to arbitration:
- Cheaper than court
- No jury trials
- Class actions waived
- You pick the venue

### 5. **Indemnification**
Users must defend you if:
- They misuse the app
- They violate the terms
- Their content causes harm
- They break the law

### 6. **AS-IS Disclaimer**
App provided without warranties:
- No guarantee it meets their needs
- No guarantee it's error-free
- No guarantee of results
- Users accept all risks

## 🚨 Red Flags to Discuss with Your Lawyer

1. **Class Action Waiver** - Some provinces may not enforce
2. **Arbitration Clause** - May need opt-out provisions
3. **No Refund Policy** - Consumer protection laws may override
4. **Liability Cap** - May be challenged in court
5. **Governing Law** - Users in other provinces/countries

**Have your lawyer review these specific clauses.**
